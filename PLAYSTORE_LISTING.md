## Full Description

Dot Hunter is a fast-paced focus game that tests your reflexes, perception, and concentration. Dots appear on screen — tap them before they vanish. Sounds simple? It gets faster. Every. Single. Round.

THREE GAME MODES

Classic Focus — One dot appears at a time. Tap it before the timer runs out. Pure speed, zero distractions. The window to react shrinks every round.

Color Filter — Green dots are your target. But red, orange, purple, and other colors flood the screen as decoys. One wrong tap costs you. Can you filter the noise?

Dual Hunt — Choose two colors before the game starts. A cluster of mixed-color dots spawns each wave. Find your targets in the chaos. Waves grow larger and faster as you progress.

FEATURES

- Escalating difficulty that keeps pushing your limits
- 16 unlockable achievements across bronze, silver, and gold tiers
- Persistent high scores and game history
- Light and dark themes
- Clean, modern design built for mobile
- Original synthesized sound effects
- Ready screen with instructions before every round

PRIVACY FIRST

No accounts. No sign-ins. No analytics. No ads. All your data stays on your device. Period.
